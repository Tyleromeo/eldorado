Trend over time
Estimates derived from your data, not a lab or wearable measurement. Daniels' formula uses your threshold pace setting; the HR-based figure uses your highest observed max heart rate from recent runs and your resting HR setting. The trend line recalculates VDOT week-by-week from your fastest sustained run pace, so it reflects actual changes in run performance over time.
